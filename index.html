Frequently Asked Questions

How does the audio detection work?

Fretio uses your device's microphone to detect the notes you play on your guitar. Make sure to grant microphone permissions when prompted and play in a quiet environment for best results.

Do I need an acoustic or electric guitar?

Both acoustic and electric guitars work with Fretio. For electric guitars, you can play unplugged or through an amp - the app will detect the sound from your device's microphone.

What skill level is this app suitable for?

Fretio is designed for all skill levels, from beginners learning basic note positions to advanced players working on complex chord progressions and music theory.

Can I use this app without a guitar?

Yes! You can practice by tapping the fretboard interface even without a physical guitar. The app includes audio playback for each note and chord.
